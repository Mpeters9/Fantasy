Fantasy Football Guides

Beginner Tips

  • Draft a balanced roster: Prioritize a QB, RB, and WR in early rounds.
  • Monitor the waiver wire for breakout players each week.
  • Use the Trade Analyzer to evaluate trade fairness.
  • Check Matchup Predictor for weekly lineup decisions.

Advanced Strategies

  • Stack players from the same team (e.g., QB and WR) for higher points.
  • Target players with favorable playoff schedules (Weeks 15-17).
  • Use Player Comparison to scout trades or waiver pickups.
